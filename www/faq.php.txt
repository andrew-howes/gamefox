### Answers

#### How do I open the options window? {#q1}

There are two ways to open the options window:

1. Open the |Tools| menu and choose |GameFOX...|. This is the quickest way.
2. Open the |Tools| menu and go to |Add-ons|. Find GameFOX in the add-on list
and click the |Options| or |Preferences| button.
